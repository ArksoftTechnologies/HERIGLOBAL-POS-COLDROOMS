# Heriglobal POS User Manual

## Getting Started
1. **Login**: Access the system using your username and password.
2. **Dashboard**: You will land on a dashboard tailored to your role.

## For Sales Representatives
### Processing a Sale
1. Go to **POS** in the sidebar.
2. Search for products by name or SKU.
3. Click "Add to Cart" or update quantity.
4. Select a **Customer** (default is Walk-In).
5. Review the **Total Amount**.
6. Select **Payment Mode** (Cash, Credit, Transfer).
7. If Credit, ensure customer has sufficient limit.
8. Click **Complete Sale**.

### Handling Returns
1. Go to **Sales History**.
2. Find the relevant sale and click "View".
3. Click "Process Return".
4. Enter quantity to return for each item.
5. Mark as **Resellable** or **Damaged**.
6. Confirm return.

### Recording Expenses
1. Go to **Expenses** > **Record Expense**.
2. Enter amount, date, category, and description.
3. Save.

## For Outlet Admins
### Receiving Stock
1. Go to **Inventory** > **Transfers**.
2. View pending transfers from Warehouse.
3. Verify physical count.
4. Click **Receive**.

### Managing Users
1. (Limited access) View staff performance in Reports.

## Remittance
1. **Declare Collection**: Record cash/transfer totals at end of shift.
2. **Record Remittance**: Log payments sent to head office.
3. **Check Outstanding**: Ensure your balance is zero.
